Action for Animal Health

The prevalence and spread of infectious animal diseases pose significant threats to livestock health, food security, and economic stability in agricultural communities. Addressing these diseases requires comprehensive strategies that include effective vaccination programs, enhanced biosecurity measures, and improved veterinary care. However, limited resources, inadequate infrastructure, and lack of education among farmers hinder these efforts, leading to persistent outbreaks and significant financial losses. This problem highlights the urgent need for coordinated actions to improve disease management and promote sustainable agricultural practices.
